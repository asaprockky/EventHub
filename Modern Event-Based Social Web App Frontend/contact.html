Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event?

Simply sign up for an account, click "Create Event" in the navigation, and fill out the event details form. Your event will be live once you submit it.

Is EventHub free to use?

Yes! EventHub is completely free for both event organizers and attendees. We believe in making connections accessible to everyone.

How do you ensure event safety?

We have community guidelines, user verification systems, and a reporting mechanism. We also provide safety tips for meeting new people.

Can I cancel my event attendance?

Yes, you can leave an event at any time before it starts. Just go to the event page and click "Leave Event".

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

You can report users or events using the report button on their profiles or event pages. We take all reports seriously.

Can I organize private events?

Yes! When creating an event, you can set it as private and invite specific people rather than making it publicly discoverable.
